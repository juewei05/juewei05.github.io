About Me

Hi, I am Jue Wei(韦珏).

I am a senior undergraduate student majoring in Quantum Information Science at the School of the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My research interests primarily lie in Non-Light-of-Sight Imaging.

I am currently working on Passive Non-Line-of-Sight Imaging Based on Natural Scene Encoding under the supervision of Prof. Feihu Xu(徐飞虎).

Feel free to contact me via email: juewei [at] ustc.edu.
